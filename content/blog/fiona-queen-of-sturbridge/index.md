---
title: Fiona, Queen of Sturbridge
date: "2017-05-02"
category: ["poetry"]
---

<br/>

IT IS WITH REGRET,

THAT WE CANNOT ACCEPT,

YOUR INVITATION TO SAY,

FIONA, HAPPPY BIRTHDAY.

<br/>

WE ARE HAPPY TO GO,

OUT TO CHICAGO,

TO CONGRATULATE OUR GRANDSON,

ON A GOAL HARD WON,

HIS GRADUATION.

<br/>

BUD ROSCH,
FIRST STURBRIDGE POET LAUREATE
